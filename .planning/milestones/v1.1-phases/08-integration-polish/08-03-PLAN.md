  <how-to-verify>
    Open the project in Godot 4.2+ and press F5 to run.

    **Check 1 — HUD right-click with occupied weapon slot:**
    1. Pick up a weapon item from the world.
    2. Open inventory (Tab), right-click the weapon in the bag, select "Equip". Close inventory.
    3. Right-click the weapon slot in the HUD strip (bottom of screen).
    Expected: A popup appears with exactly two items: "Unequip" and "Drop".

    **Check 2 — HUD right-click with inventory panel closed:**
    1. Ensure a weapon is equipped (HUD weapon slot shows an icon).
    2. Make sure the inventory panel is CLOSED.
    3. Right-click the weapon slot in the HUD strip.
    Expected: The popup still appears. The inventory being closed must not prevent the HUD menu from showing.

    **Check 3 — Unequip from HUD:**
    1. With a weapon equipped, open the HUD right-click menu.
    2. Select "Unequip".
    Expected: The weapon disappears from the HUD slot and reappears in the bag grid (open inventory to confirm). HUD slot becomes empty/dim.

    **Check 4 — Drop from HUD:**
    1. Equip a weapon again.
    2. Right-click HUD weapon slot, select "Drop".
    Expected: The weapon disappears from the HUD slot. A collectable item appears near the player in the world. Bag is NOT affected (item does not go to bag).

    **Check 5 — Empty HUD slot right-click:**
    1. Ensure the weapon slot is empty (nothing equipped).
    2. Right-click the weapon slot in the HUD strip.
    Expected: No menu appears. Nothing happens.

    **Check 6 — Viewport-edge popup positioning:**
    1. Move the mouse to a corner of the screen (top-right or bottom-right).
    2. Right-click an occupied HUD slot or an occupied bag slot while the inventory is open.
    Expected: The popup appears fully within the viewport — no part of it is clipped or outside the screen edge.

    **Check 7 — Popup lifecycle on inventory close:**
    1. Open inventory (Tab).
    2. Right-click a bag slot to open a context menu (do not select an item — leave the menu open).
    3. Close inventory (Tab again).
    Expected: The context menu disappears cleanly. No errors in the Godot Output panel.
  </how-to-verify>
